<p><p>The Dynamic Wireless EV Charging System market comprises three key components: the Base Charging Pad (Transmitter), Power Control Unit, and Vehicle Charging Pad (Receiver). The Base Charging Pad, installed on roads or parking spaces, transmits energy wirelessly. The Power Control Unit manages energy flow, ensuring efficient and safe charging. The Vehicle Charging Pad, integrated into EVs, receives the transmitted energy to charge the battery. Together, these components enable seamless, on-the-go charging, enhancing convenience and reducing reliance on traditional charging infrastructure.</p></p>
