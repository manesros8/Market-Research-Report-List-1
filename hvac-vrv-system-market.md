<p><p>The HVAC VRV (Variable Refrigerant Volume) system market primarily includes two main types: heat recovery systems and heat pump systems. Heat recovery systems allow concurrent heating and cooling in different zones, efficiently redistributing excess heat, which enhances energy savings. In contrast, heat pump systems provide either heating or cooling, depending on the demand. Both systems aim to optimize energy use and maintain comfortable indoor climates, contributing to the growing trend toward energy-efficient HVAC solutions.</p></p>
