<p><strong>The Semi-Automatic Cartoning Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>V-FFS</li><li>H-FFS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-automatic cartoning machine market primarily encompasses two types: Vertical Form-Fill-Seal (V-FFS) and Horizontal Form-Fill-Seal (H-FFS) machines. V-FFS machines create and fill cartons from a roll of film in a vertical orientation, suited for smaller, lightweight products and offering high-speed operations. In contrast, H-FFS machines operate horizontally, forming and filling cartons from flat sheets, ideal for bulkier items. Both types enhance packaging efficiency and streamline operations in various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods.</p></p>
