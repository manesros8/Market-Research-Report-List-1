<p><p>Catalyst Coated Membranes (CCMs) are essential components in fuel cells, facilitating electrochemical reactions. The **3-layer CCM** consists of a proton exchange membrane sandwiched between two catalyst layers, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. The **5-layer CCM** adds gas diffusion layers on both sides, enhancing performance and durability by improving reactant distribution and water management. The **Other market** includes specialized or customized CCMs tailored for unique applications, such as high-temperature or low-humidity environments, catering to niche requirements beyond standard 3-layer or 5-layer configurations. Each type serves distinct performance and application needs.</p></p>
