<p><ul><li>One-component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adhesive accelerator market is divided into two main types: one-component and two-component systems. One-component adhesives are pre-formulated for easy use, requiring no mixing, which simplifies application and reduces preparation time. In contrast, two-component systems require the mixing of an accelerant with a base adhesive just before application, enhancing bonding strength and curing speed. Typically, two-component adhesives provide superior performance in high-stress applications, while one-component adhesives are favored for convenience and faster application in less demanding environments.</p></p>
