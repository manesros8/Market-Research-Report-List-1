<p><p>The infusion tube heater market is segmented into two main types: constant temperature and variable temperature heaters. Constant temperature heaters maintain a fixed temperature, ensuring consistent thermal output for specific medical applications, promoting precision and reliability. In contrast, variable temperature heaters allow for adjustable settings, accommodating a range of infusion temperatures and diverse patient needs. This flexibility is essential in dynamic medical environments where different treatments may require varying thermal conditions. Both types aim to enhance patient safety and optimize infusion processes.</p></p>
