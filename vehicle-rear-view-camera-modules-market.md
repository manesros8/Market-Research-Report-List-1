<p><p>Vehicle rear-view camera modules enhance safety by providing clear visibility of the rear surroundings. The market is primarily segmented into CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras offer superior image quality, especially in low-light conditions, making them ideal for premium vehicles. CMOS cameras, on the other hand, are cost-effective, energy-efficient, and widely used in mass-market vehicles due to their compact design and faster processing capabilities. Both types cater to diverse automotive needs, balancing performance, affordability, and integration ease.</p></p>
