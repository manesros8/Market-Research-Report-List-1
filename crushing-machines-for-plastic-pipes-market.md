<p><p>Crushing machines for plastic pipes can be categorized into vertical and horizontal types. Vertical crushing machines utilize a top-down approach, relying on gravity to assist in the crushing process, making them compact and suitable for limited space environments. In contrast, horizontal crushing machines operate with a side-to-side movement, providing a more extensive feeding capacity and greater efficiency for larger volumes of plastic pipes. Both types serve the crucial function of recycling and reducing waste in the plastic industry.</p></p>
