<p><p>The rubber compound market comprises several key types based on material composition. EPDM compounding utilizes ethylene propylene diene monomer for durability and weather resistance. SBR compounding features styrene-butadiene rubber, ideal for tire manufacturing. BR compounding, or butadiene rubber, offers high resilience and elasticity. Natural rubber compounding focuses on using latex from rubber trees for its excellent tensile strength. NBR compounding incorporates nitrile rubber, known for oil resistance. Silicone rubber compounding provides heat and chemical resilience. Other specialized compounds cater to niche applications.</p></p>
