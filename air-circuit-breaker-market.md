<p><strong>The Air Circuit Breaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Blast Circuit Breaker</li><li>Plain Air Circuit Breaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air circuit breaker market consists of various types, primarily including Air Blast Circuit Breakers and Plain Air Circuit Breakers. Air Blast Circuit Breakers use a stream of high-pressure air to extinguish the arc formed during circuit interruption, making them suitable for high voltage applications. In contrast, Plain Air Circuit Breakers operate by using ambient air as the medium for arc extinction, typically in lower voltage settings. Both types are essential for providing protection and control in electrical distribution systems.</p></p>
