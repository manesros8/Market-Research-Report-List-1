<p><p>The PVC compound stabilizer market encompasses various forms, primarily characterized by white flake and white powder. White flake stabilizers are often preferred for their ease of handling and blending properties in PVC production, offering good performance in processing and end-use applications. In contrast, white powder stabilizers provide a fine particle size, which can enhance dispersion and compatibility within the PVC matrix. Both types play a crucial role in improving thermal stability, prolonging product lifespan, and maintaining quality in PVC formulations.</p></p>
