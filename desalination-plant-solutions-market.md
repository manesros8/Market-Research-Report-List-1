<p><p>The desalination plant solutions market includes various techniques to convert seawater into potable water. Reverse Osmosis (RO) is the most prevalent method, using semi-permeable membranes to separate salt and impurities from water. Multi-Effect Distillation (MED) relies on thermal processes, evaporating and condensing water multiple times to enhance efficiency. Other technologies encompass methods like Electrodialysis and Solar Desalination, each catering to specific needs and environments. Together, these solutions address the growing demand for freshwater in arid regions and stressed water resources.</p></p>
