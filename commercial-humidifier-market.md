<p><p>The commercial humidifier market includes various types, prominently vapor type and water spray humidifiers. Vapor type humidifiers generate steam, providing warm humidity that helps maintain indoor air quality in large spaces. They are often used in environments requiring sterility, like hospitals. In contrast, water spray humidifiers utilize fine mist to increase moisture levels without heating, making them ideal for industrial settings where temperature control is crucial. Both types improve air comfort and protect materials and equipment from drying out.</p></p>
