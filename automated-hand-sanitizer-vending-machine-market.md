<p><p>The Automated Hand Sanitizer Vending Machine market comprises three primary types: liquid, foam, and gel. Foam sanitizers, on the other hand, deliver a lathering effect that allows for more thorough coverage and may be perceived as less wasteful. Gel sanitizers offer a viscous texture that is easy to apply while ensuring effective germ elimination. Each type caters to different user preferences and applications in public spaces.</p></p>
