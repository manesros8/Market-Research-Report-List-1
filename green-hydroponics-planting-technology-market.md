<p><p>Green hydroponics planting technology encompasses various market types, notably tiled and vertical hydroponics. Tiled hydroponics involves the use of flat, modular systems where plants grow in nutrient-rich solutions spread across tiled surfaces, optimizing space and resource efficiency. In contrast, vertical hydroponics maximizes land use by stacking plant rows vertically, supported by structured systems, which enhances light exposure and crop yield. Both methods promote sustainable agriculture by conserving water and reducing land requirements while enabling year-round cultivation.</p></p>
