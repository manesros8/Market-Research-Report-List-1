<p><p>The vehicle door lockset market comprises several types, primarily including pin tumbler locks, wafer tumbler locks, and other variants. Pin tumbler locks use a set of pins that align to permit the lock to turn, offering high security and durability. Wafer tumbler locks utilize flat wafers instead of pins, making them simpler and generally more cost-effective, though less secure. Other types may include electronic locks and smart locks, which incorporate advanced technology for enhanced security and convenience.</p></p>
