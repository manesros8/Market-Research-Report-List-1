<p><p>Dissolved acetylene cylinders are specialized gas containers used to store acetylene gas in a stable, pressurized form, typically dissolved in a solvent, such as acetone or dimethylformamide. This ensures safe handling and transport, as pure acetylene is highly volatile and flammable. These cylinders are primarily utilized in various applications, including welding, cutting, and lighting industries, where high-temperature flames are required.</p><p>The Dissolved Acetylene Cylinder Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.5% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand in construction and manufacturing sectors.
