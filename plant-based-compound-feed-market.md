<p><p>The plant-based compound feed market includes various forms tailored for livestock nutrition. **Mash** is a finely ground mixture of raw materials, offering flexibility and ease of digestion. **Pellets** are compressed and heat-treated feeds, providing durability, reduced waste, and improved nutrient absorption. **Crumbles** are broken pellets, ideal for young or small animals due to their smaller size and digestibility. **Other forms** include extruded or textured feeds, designed for specific dietary needs or animal preferences, ensuring balanced nutrition and efficient feeding practices.</p></p>
