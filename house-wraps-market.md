<p><p>House wraps are essential in the construction industry, offering various types to enhance building performance. Asphalt-impregnated paper or fiberglass provides moisture resistance and air infiltration protection. Micro-perforated wraps allow for moisture vapor transmission while blocking liquid water. Spunbonded nonwoven materials combine strength and breathability, ideal for diverse climates. Woven house wraps offer durability and resistance to tearing. Drainable wraps facilitate moisture drainage, reducing the risk of mold and enhancing wall assembly durability. These variations cater to specific building needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
