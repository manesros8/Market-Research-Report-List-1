<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Underwater Acoustic Positioning System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultra-short Baseline(USBL)</li><li>Short Baseline(SBL)</li><li>Long Baseline(LBL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underwater acoustic positioning system market consists of three primary types: Ultra-short Baseline (USBL), Short Baseline (SBL), and Long Baseline (LBL). USBL uses a single transducer to determine a target's position based on time of flight and angle, suitable for shallow waters. SBL employs multiple receivers for better accuracy in limited areas. LBL utilizes multiple fixed transponders on the seafloor, providing high precision over longer distances, making it ideal for deep-sea applications and extensive underwater surveys.</p></p>
