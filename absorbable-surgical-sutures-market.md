<p><strong>The Absorbable Surgical Sutures Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monofilament Sutures</li><li>Multifilament Sutures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The absorbable surgical sutures market is categorized into monofilament and multifilament sutures. Monofilament sutures consist of a single strand, offering smooth passage through tissue and reduced tissue drag, which minimizes infection risk. They are less pliable but provide better tensile strength. In contrast, multifilament sutures consist of multiple strands braided together, enhancing flexibility and handling but may increase tissue drag and infection risk. Both types are designed to dissolve in the body over time, eliminating the need for removal.</p></p>
