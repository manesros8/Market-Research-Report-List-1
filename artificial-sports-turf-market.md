<p><p>The artificial sports turf market consists of three primary types: polypropylene turf, polyethylene turf, and nylon turf. Polypropylene turf is lightweight and cost-effective but less durable, making it suitable for temporary installations. Polyethylene turf offers a more realistic feel and is commonly used in football and soccer fields due to its durability and softness. Nylon turf is the most robust option, providing excellent resilience and longevity, making it ideal for high-traffic sports like field hockey and baseball.</p></p>
