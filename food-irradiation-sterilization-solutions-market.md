<p><p>The Food Irradiation Sterilization Solutions Market encompasses various technologies used to enhance food safety and shelf life. Gamma radiation utilizes cobalt or cesium isotopes to penetrate food, effectively killing bacteria and parasites. X-ray radiation employs high-energy photons for sterilization, providing deeper penetration and less residual radioactivity. Electron beam radiation uses accelerated electrons for rapid surface treatment, offering precise dosage control. Each method effectively reduces pathogens while maintaining food quality, catering to the growing demand for safe, long-lasting food products.</p></p>
