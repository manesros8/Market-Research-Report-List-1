<p><p>Exoskeleton robots are categorized into three main types: lower body, upper body, and full body. Lower body exoskeletons assist users in walking or lifting by providing support to the legs, making them valuable for rehabilitation and mobility enhancement. Upper body exoskeletons focus on assisting the arms and shoulders, often used in industrial settings to reduce strain during lifting tasks. Full body exoskeletons combine both functionalities, aiding overall mobility and strength, appealing to medical recovery and occupational applications.</p></p>
