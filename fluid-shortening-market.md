<p><p>Fluid shortening markets are categorized into three types: Normal Type, Stable Type, and High Emulsification Type. Normal Type is characterized by basic functionality suitable for standard baking needs. Stable Type offers enhanced resistance to temperature and structure changes, ideal for products requiring consistency over time. High Emulsification Type excels in blending oil and water, providing superior texture and moisture retention, making it perfect for products like cakes and whipped toppings. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance requirements.</p></p>
