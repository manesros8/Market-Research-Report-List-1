<p><ul><li>High Temperature Vulcanization(HTV)</li><li>Room Temperature Vulcanization(RTV)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicone rubber market is categorized primarily into High Temperature Vulcanization (HTV) and Room Temperature Vulcanization (RTV) types. HTV silicone rubber is processed through heat, resulting in durable and high-performance products suitable for extreme temperatures and demanding applications. In contrast, RTV silicone rubber cures at room temperature, making it convenient for general use and cost-effective for various applications, including adhesives and sealants. Both types cater to diverse industries, ensuring versatility in usage based on specific performance requirements.</p></p>
