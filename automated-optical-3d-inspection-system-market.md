<p><p>The Automated Optical 3D Inspection System market is divided into two main types: online and offline systems. Online systems integrate directly into manufacturing processes, enabling real-time inspection of parts as they are produced, ensuring immediate quality control and reducing downtime. Conversely, offline systems operate separately from production lines, allowing for batch inspections on finished products. This approach is often used for detailed evaluation and troubleshooting, providing more comprehensive analysis at the expense of immediate feedback.</p></p>
