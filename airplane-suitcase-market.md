<p><p>The airplane suitcase market is divided into two main types: soft and hard suitcases. Soft suitcases are made from flexible materials like fabric, allowing for more expansion and easier storage but may offer less protection. They are often lighter and come with multiple pockets for organization. In contrast, hard suitcases are constructed from durable materials like polycarbonate or ABS, providing enhanced protection against impact and moisture. They typically feature a sleek design and are preferred for their security, especially for fragile contents.</p></p>
