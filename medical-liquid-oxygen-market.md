<p><p>The medical liquid oxygen market is primarily segmented into two types: on-site gas and bottled gas. On-site gas involves the production of oxygen at healthcare facilities using cryogenic processes, ensuring a continuous supply and reducing transportation costs. Bottled gas, on the other hand, involves the storage of liquid oxygen in high-pressure cylinders for distribution to hospitals and clinics. Both types cater to the diverse needs of medical applications, enhancing patient care and supporting various therapeutic treatments.</p></p>
