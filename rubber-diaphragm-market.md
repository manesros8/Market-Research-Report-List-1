<p><p>The rubber diaphragm market comprises various types, each serving specific applications. Flat diaphragms provide a simple, flexible barrier ideal for low-pressure systems. Rolling diaphragms offer extended travel and are used in pumps and actuators. Dish-shaped diaphragms are effective in pressure vessels for their ability to handle high pressure. Convoluted diaphragms, with their folds, are designed for maximum flexibility and minimal space usage. Other types may include custom designs catering to niche applications, enhancing versatility in industrial use.</p></p>
