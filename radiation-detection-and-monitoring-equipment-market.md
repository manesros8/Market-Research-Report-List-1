<p><p>Radiation detection and monitoring equipment encompasses various devices designed to measure and manage exposure to ionizing radiation. Personal dosimeters assess individual radiation exposure, ensuring worker safety. Area process monitors evaluate radiation levels in specific locations, crucial for facility oversight. Environmental radiation monitors measure background radiation in air, water, and soil to safeguard public health. Surface contamination monitors detect radioactive particles on surfaces, while radioactive material monitors ensure secure handling and transport of radioactive substances, enhancing safety protocols across diverse sectors.</p></p>
