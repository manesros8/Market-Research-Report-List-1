<p><p>The bicycle inner tube valve market is primarily divided into two types: rubber tire valves and metal tire valves. Rubber tire valves, typically the Schrader type, are lightweight, affordable, and commonly found in standard bicycles, offering ease of use and maintenance. Metal tire valves, often in the Presta style, are more durable, provide a tighter seal, and are favored for performance bicycles due to their ability to hold higher pressure. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences in cycling.</p></p>
