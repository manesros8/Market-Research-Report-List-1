<p><p>Minor Procedure Kits and Trays are categorized into two main types: disposable and non-disposable. Disposable kits are designed for single-use, ensuring hygiene and reducing the risk of cross-contamination in clinical settings. They are convenient and often cost-effective for facilities with high turnover. In contrast, non-disposable kits are reusable, made from durable materials, and require thorough sterilization between uses. While they can lead to savings over time, they demand more stringent maintenance protocols to ensure safety and efficacy in procedures.</p></p>
