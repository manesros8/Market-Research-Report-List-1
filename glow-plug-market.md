<p><p>The glow plug market is primarily categorized into metal and ceramic types. Metal glow plugs are typically made from steel or copper and provide rapid heating, making them popular for diesel engines. They are cost-effective but can have shorter lifespans due to wear. In contrast, ceramic glow plugs offer enhanced durability and thermal efficiency, heating quickly and maintaining their temperature longer. Although they come at a higher price, their longevity and performance make them suitable for modern high-performance engines.</p></p>
