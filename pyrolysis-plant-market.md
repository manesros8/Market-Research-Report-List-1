<p><p>The pyrolysis plant market comprises three main types: batch, semi-continuous, and fully continuous. Batch-type plants process materials in discrete loads, suitable for smaller volumes and flexible operations. Semi-continuous systems allow for partial continuous feed, improving efficiency while still accommodating some operational variability. Fully continuous plants operate continuously, maximizing throughput and automation, ideal for large-scale operations. Each type serves different production needs and scales, catering to varying industrial applications and efficiencies in converting waste into valuable products.</p></p>
