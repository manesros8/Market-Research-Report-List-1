<p><ul><li>Single Level Ventilators</li><li>Bilevel Ventilators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home non-invasive ventilator market comprises two main types: single-level and bilevel ventilators. Single-level ventilators provide a constant airflow, ideal for conditions requiring steady support, such as obstructive sleep apnea. Bilevel ventilators, on the other hand, offer two distinct pressure levels for inhalation and exhalation, making them suitable for patients with more complex respiratory issues like chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD). Both types enhance patient comfort and facilitate effective ventilation at home, promoting better healthcare management.</p></p>
