<p><p>The ball bearing materials market consists of three primary types: steel, ceramic, and plastic. Steel bearings, known for their strength and durability, are widely used in industrial applications. Ceramic bearings offer higher performance with lower friction and are ideal for high-speed or corrosive environments, making them suitable for aerospace and medical devices. Plastic bearings are lightweight, resistant to chemicals, and suitable for applications with lower loads and speeds, often used in consumer products and automated machinery.</p></p>
