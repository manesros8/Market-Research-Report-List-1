<p><p>A Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC) Converter is an emissions control device used in diesel engines to reduce harmful pollutants such as carbon monoxide (CO), hydrocarbons (HC), and particulate matter (PM). It works by oxidizing these pollutants into less harmful substances like carbon dioxide (CO2) and water (H2O) through a chemical reaction facilitated by a catalyst, typically made of platinum or palladium. DOCs are a critical component in meeting stringent emission regulations worldwide.</p><p>The Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC) Converter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period.
