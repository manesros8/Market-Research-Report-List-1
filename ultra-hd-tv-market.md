<p><p>The Ultra-HD TV market comprises primarily two types: LCD and OLED televisions. LCD TVs utilize liquid crystal displays to produce vibrant images, often enhanced with LED backlighting for improved contrast and brightness. In contrast, OLED TVs leverage organic compounds that emit light, resulting in deeper blacks and superior color accuracy. While LCDs are typically more affordable and widely available, OLEDs offer a premium viewing experience with greater picture quality, attracting consumers seeking advanced display technology.</p></p>
