<p><ul><li>Steam Dried(SD)</li><li>Flame Dried(FD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fish meal feed market is primarily categorized into two types: Steam Dried (SD) and Flame Dried (FD). SD fish meal is produced using steam heat, resulting in a high-quality product with better nutritional retention and lower oxidation levels. In contrast, FD fish meal is generated through direct exposure to flame, offering a cost-effective method but potentially lower nutritional quality. Both types serve diverse applications in aquaculture and livestock feeds, catering to varying dietary needs and budget considerations.</p></p>
