<p><p>The open motor yachts market is primarily categorized into two types based on hull design: planing hulls and displacement hulls. Planing hulls are designed for high speeds, allowing the yacht to rise above the water surface, providing quicker navigation and agility. Conversely, displacement hulls move through the water at slower speeds, offering superior stability and efficiency over longer distances. Each type caters to different preferences and usage, affecting performance, cruising comfort, and overall boating experience.</p></p>
