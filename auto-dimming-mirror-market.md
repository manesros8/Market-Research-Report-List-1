<p><strong>The Auto-Dimming Mirror Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Electrodynamic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The auto-dimming mirror market features two primary types: mechanical and electrodynamic. Mechanical mirrors utilize a liquid-filled chamber with a prismatic effect, adjusting tint based on light exposure. These mirrors are generally less expensive and simpler in design. In contrast, electrodynamic mirrors employ complex electrochromic technology, enabling more responsive and precise dimming. This type offers enhanced performance and aesthetics, making it popular in high-end vehicles. Both types aim to improve driver visibility and reduce glare from headlights.</p></p>
