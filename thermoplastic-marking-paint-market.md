<p><p>Thermoplastic marking paint is a durable, solvent-based material used for road markings, pedestrian crossings, and other traffic-related applications. It is composed of synthetic resins, plasticizers, pigments, and glass beads for reflectivity. The material is heated to a molten state before application, forming a strong bond with the surface upon cooling. Its high durability, weather resistance, and retro-reflective properties make it a preferred choice for road safety and traffic management.</p><p>The Thermoplastic Marking Paint Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.8% during the forecast period. Governments and private sectors are investing heavily in road construction and maintenance, further boosting demand. Overall, the market is poised for robust growth, driven by technological advancements and increasing safety regulations.</p></p>
