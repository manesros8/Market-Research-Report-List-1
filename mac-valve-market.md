<p><p>The MAC Valve market encompasses several key types: 3-way valves, 4-way valves, bullet valves, and pulse valves. 3-way valves control fluid flow through three ports, allowing for mixing or diverting. 4-way valves manage flow in multiple directions, often used in actuators. Bullet valves provide compact solutions for on-off control, whereas pulse valves are designed for quick actuation in applications like dust collection systems. Together, these components cater to diverse industrial automation and fluid control needs.</p></p>
