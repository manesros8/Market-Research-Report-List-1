<p><strong>The I-line Photoresist Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photopolymerization</li><li>Light Decomposition Type</li><li>Photocrosslinked</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The I-line photoresist market is divided into three main types: photopolymerization, light decomposition, and photocrosslinked. Photopolymerization involves the creation of polymer structures through UV light exposure, allowing for precise patterning in semiconductor applications. Light decomposition photoresists rely on chemical reactions initiated by light to break down components, resulting in enhanced pattern resolution. Photocrosslinked photoresists form stable crosslinked networks upon exposure, providing improved thermal and chemical resistance, essential for advanced lithography processes in microelectronics manufacturing.</p></p>
