<p><strong>The Monolithic Photonic Integrated Circuit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Photonic Integrated Circuit</li><li>Active Photonic Integrated Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The monolithic photonic integrated circuit market is divided into two main types: passive and active photonic integrated circuits. Passive photonic integrated circuits encompass components like waveguides, filters, and splitters that do not require external power for operation, primarily used for signal manipulation. In contrast, active photonic integrated circuits incorporate elements such as lasers, modulators, and photodetectors, requiring external power to perform functions like signal generation and amplification. Both types are crucial for advancing photonic technologies in telecommunications, sensing, and data processing.</p></p>
