<p><p>Transparent barrier packaging films have distinct types, each offering unique properties for various applications. Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) is known for its excellent clarity and moisture barrier, making it ideal for food packaging. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) provides strong chemical resistance and flexibility, suitable for protective packaging. Polylactic Acid (PLA) is a biodegradable option, appealing for eco-friendly products. Polyethylene (PE) is versatile and widely used for its durability and moisture resistance, commonly found in flexible packaging solutions.</p></p>
