<p><strong>The Packaging Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Hot-melt based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The packaging adhesives market comprises three key types: water-based, solvent-based, and hot-melt adhesives. Water-based adhesives utilize water as a solvent, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, offering strong adhesion but posing VOC concerns. Hot-melt adhesives are thermoplastic materials that, when heated, become tacky and bond quickly upon cooling, providing strong bonds and excellent performance in various temperature conditions. Each type serves specific applications in the packaging industry, catering to diverse material needs.</p></p>
