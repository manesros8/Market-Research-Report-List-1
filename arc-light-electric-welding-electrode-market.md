<p><strong>The Arc Light Electric Welding Electrode Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Consumable Welding Electrode</li><li>Non-consumable Welding Electrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Arc Light Electric Welding Electrode market is divided into two main types: consumable and non-consumable welding electrodes. Consumable electrodes are melted during the welding process and contribute to the weld pool, providing filler material. They are commonly used in processes like MIG and TIG welding. Non-consumable electrodes, on the other hand, do not melt and are used primarily in welding techniques such as TIG welding, where they maintain a consistent arc while preserving the material's integrity.</p></p>
