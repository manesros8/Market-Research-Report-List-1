<p><p>The brain cancer molecular diagnostics market encompasses several advanced technologies for accurate tumor characterization. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies specific DNA segments, enabling detection of genetic mutations. FISH (Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization) visualizes specific DNA sequences within cells, aiding in chromosomal analysis. DNA sequencing determines the exact order of nucleotides in a DNA segment, providing comprehensive insights into genetic alterations. Gene chips, or microarrays, analyze thousands of genes simultaneously to identify expression patterns and mutations, facilitating personalized treatment strategies for brain cancer patients.</p></p>
