<p><strong>The Photosensitive Polyimide Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photosensitive Polyimide</li><li>Negative Photosensitive Polyimide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photosensitive polyimide coatings market is divided into two main types: positive and negative photosensitive polyimides. Positive photosensitive polyimides become soluble when exposed to light, allowing for the creation of intricate patterns upon development, making them ideal for microelectronics and semiconductor applications. In contrast, negative photosensitive polyimides become insoluble upon exposure, leading to robust and durable structures that are widely used in flexible electronics and various MEMS devices. Both types are essential for advanced manufacturing processes requiring precision and reliability.</p></p>
