<p><p>The Precision Pipetting System market is divided into two main types: multi-channel and single-channel pipetting systems. Single-channel pipettes are designed for highly accurate dispensing of liquids in small volumes, ideal for tasks requiring precision in limited samples. In contrast, multi-channel pipettes enable simultaneous dispensing across multiple wells, enhancing efficiency in high-throughput environments, such as in laboratories and research settings. Both types cater to varying needs in precision and throughput, essential for scientific experimentation and analysis.</p></p>
