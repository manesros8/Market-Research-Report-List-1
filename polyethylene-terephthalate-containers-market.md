<p><p>The Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) containers market comprises various manufacturing processes, each suited for specific applications. Stretch blow molding produces lightweight, strong bottles by stretching pre-formed PET parisons, ideal for beverages. Extrusion blow molding creates hollow containers by extruding molten PET and inflating it within a mold, suited for larger items like industrial containers. Thermoforming involves heating PET sheets and forming them over molds, commonly used for packaging trays and clamshells, offering versatility in design and applications.</p></p>
