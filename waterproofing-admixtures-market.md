<p><strong>The Waterproofing Admixtures Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crystalline Type</li><li>Pore Blocking Type</li><li>Other Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterproofing admixtures market comprises three main types: crystalline, pore blocking, and other types. Crystalline types contain materials that catalyze a reaction to form insoluble crystals within concrete, enhancing its impermeability over time. Pore blocking types include compounds that create a barrier, preventing water from penetrating through existing pores. Other types encompass various additives that provide additional waterproofing properties or enhance performance, such as elastomeric, hydrophobic, or surface-applied treatments, to meet specific project requirements.</p></p>
