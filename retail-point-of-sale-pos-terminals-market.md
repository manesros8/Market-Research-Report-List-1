<p><p>The Retail POS Terminals market comprises two main types: Fixed Retail POS Terminals and Mobile Retail POS Terminals. Fixed Retail POS Terminals are traditional, stationary systems often found in brick-and-mortar stores, featuring hardware like cash registers, barcode scanners, and receipt printers. In contrast, Mobile Retail POS Terminals are portable devices, such as tablets or smartphones, enabling sales transactions anywhere, enhancing flexibility and customer service. Both types play crucial roles in streamlining payment processing and inventory management in retail environments.</p></p>
