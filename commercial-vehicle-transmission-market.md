<p><ul><li>Manual Vehicle Transmission</li><li>Automatic Vehicle Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle transmission market comprises two main types: manual and automatic vehicle transmissions. Manual transmissions involve drivers shifting gears manually, offering greater control and typically leading to better fuel efficiency. They are preferred in applications requiring precise gear management. In contrast, automatic transmissions use hydraulic systems to change gears automatically, enhancing ease of use and driver comfort. This is particularly beneficial in urban settings with frequent stops. Both types cater to different operational needs in the commercial sector.</p></p>
