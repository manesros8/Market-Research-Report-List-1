<p><p>The metal neurovascular stent market is categorized mainly into carotid artery stents and intracranial stents. Carotid artery stents are designed for the treatment of carotid artery stenosis, enhancing blood flow to the brain and reducing stroke risk. Intracranial stents, on the other hand, cater to conditions affecting the arteries within the skull, including aneurysms and arterial occlusions. Both types focus on improving cerebral blood circulation and patient outcomes, driving innovation and development in neurovascular interventions.</p></p>
