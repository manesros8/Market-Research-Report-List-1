<p><p>The heat treatment services market encompasses various processes aimed at enhancing the properties of materials. Case hardening treatment involves hardening the surface of metals while preserving a softer core, improving wear resistance. Quenching heat treatment rapidly cools heated materials to increase hardness. Vacuum heat treatment is conducted in a vacuum environment to prevent oxidation and contamination, allowing precise control over mechanical properties. Other treatments may include annealing and tempering, each serving specific applications to optimize material performance and durability.</p></p>
