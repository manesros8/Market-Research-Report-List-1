<p><p>The booster car seat market includes three main types: backless boosters, high back boosters, and combination seats. Backless boosters are lightweight and portable, elevating the child to fit the vehicle’s seat belt properly. High back boosters provide head and neck support, ideal for vehicles lacking headrests. Combination seats serve dual functions, acting as a forward-facing harness seat for younger children and transitioning to a booster seat as they grow. Each type ensures safety while accommodating different ages and sizes.</p></p>
