<p><ul><li>AC Film Capacitor</li><li>DC Film Capacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The film dielectric capacitor market is categorized into AC film capacitors and DC film capacitors. AC film capacitors are designed for alternating current applications, offering stable performance at high frequencies and voltages, making them suitable for use in power electronics and filtering systems. In contrast, DC film capacitors are used for direct current applications, providing high capacitance, low losses, and excellent stability, ideal for energy storage and power supply circuits. Both types leverage polymer film as the dielectric material, ensuring reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
