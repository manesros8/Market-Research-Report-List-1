<p><ul><li>Enhanced Rate (EDR)</li><li>High Rate (HS/AMP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) chip market is categorized primarily into Enhanced Data Rate (EDR) and High Speed (HS/AMP) segments. EDR, useful for devices requiring a balance between speed and power consumption, supports higher data transfer rates while maintaining energy efficiency. In contrast, HS/AMP focuses on achieving even faster data transmission using an alternate transport protocol, allowing devices to leverage both Bluetooth and Wi-Fi connections. Together, these categories cater to diverse applications, enhancing performance and connectivity in various devices.</p></p>
