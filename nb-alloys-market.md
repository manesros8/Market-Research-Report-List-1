<p><p>The Nb alloys market comprises primarily two segments: ferroniobium and nickel niobium. It is essential in the manufacturing of high-performance steel for construction and automotive applications. Nickel niobium, on the other hand, is used in specialized applications such as superalloys, which demand high-temperature stability and resistance to oxidation. Both types cater to distinct industrial demands, driving innovation and demand in diverse sectors.</p></p>
