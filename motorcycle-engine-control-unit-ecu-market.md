Gasoline ECUs manage fuel injection, ignition timing, and emissions for motorcycles powered by gasoline engines, optimizing performance and efficiency. In contrast, Diesel ECUs focus on diesel-powered motorcycles, handling similar functions but tailored for the unique characteristics of diesel combustion. Both types are critical for enhancing engine performance, meeting regulatory standards, and improving fuel efficiency in their respective segments.</p></p>
