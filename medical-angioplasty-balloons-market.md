<p><p>The medical angioplasty balloons market features several types tailored for specific therapeutic needs. Normal balloons are used for straightforward dilatation of narrowed vessels. Drug-eluting balloons deliver medication to prevent restenosis while expanding the artery. Cutting balloons have tiny blades to incise plaque during dilation, effectively resizing lesions. Scoring balloons utilize controlled scoring mechanisms to facilitate vessel expansion without excessive pressure. Each type serves unique patient conditions and enhances the efficacy of angioplasty procedures in treating cardiovascular diseases.</p></p>
