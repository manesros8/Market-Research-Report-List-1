<p><p>The periodontal scalers and curettes market includes two primary types: mechanical and oscillatory instruments. Mechanical scalers utilize ultrasonic vibrations to remove plaque and calculus efficiently, providing thorough cleaning. They are often preferred for their effectiveness in deep cleaning procedures. Oscillatory devices, on the other hand, employ rapid back-and-forth motions to dislodge deposits, promoting a gentle cleaning experience. Both types cater to different preferences and clinical needs, enhancing periodontal treatments and patient comfort during dental procedures.</p></p>
