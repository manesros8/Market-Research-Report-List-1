<p><strong>The Soluble Dye Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil Soluble</li><li>Alcohol Solubility</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soluble dye market is categorized based on solvent compatibility, primarily into oil-soluble and alcohol-soluble dyes. Oil-soluble dyes are designed for applications in oil-based systems, such as plastics and coatings, providing vibrant colors without compromising viscosity. In contrast, alcohol-soluble dyes are used in formulations requiring dissolution in alcohol and alcohol-based products like inks and solvents. Both types cater to diverse industries, including textiles, food, and cosmetics, ensuring effective coloration while meeting specific solubility requirements.</p></p>
