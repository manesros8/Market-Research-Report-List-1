<p><p>The BGA solder ball market is primarily divided into two types: lead solder balls and lead-free solder balls. Lead solder balls, traditionally used for their excellent conductivity and reliability, contain lead as a primary component. However, due to health and environmental concerns, lead-free solder balls have gained popularity, utilizing materials like tin, silver, and copper. Lead-free options are increasingly mandated by regulations and preferred in various applications, including consumer electronics, where safety and sustainability are key considerations.</p></p>
