<p><p>Cesium Iodide (CsI) comes in various forms, including CsI (Tl), CsI (Na), and pure CsI, each serving distinct applications. CsI (Tl) is doped with thallium and is primarily used in scintillation detectors for medical imaging and radiation detection due to its high light yield. CsI (Na), doped with sodium, is utilized in certain types of detectors and imaging systems. Pure CsI serves as a substrate or a basic component in various applications, particularly in optoelectronics and scintillation crystals.</p></p>
