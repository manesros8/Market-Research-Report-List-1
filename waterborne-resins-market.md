<p><p>Waterborne resins are a type of polymer-based material that is dispersed in water, making them an environmentally friendly alternative to solvent-based resins. The Waterborne Resins Market is experiencing significant growth, largely driven by increasing environmental regulations and a rising demand for sustainable products. </p><p>Key industries such as automotive, construction, and furniture are adopting waterborne resins to replace traditional products, contributing to their growing popularity.
