<p><p>The beam shutter market consists of two primary types: single-blade and multiple-blade beam shutters. Single-blade beam shutters utilize one blade to control light exposure, offering simple operation and compact design, making them suitable for applications requiring quick on/off light control. In contrast, multiple-blade beam shutters feature several blades that can be opened or closed in various configurations, providing precise light modulation and extended control over exposure time, ideal for advanced imaging systems and scientific applications.</p></p>
