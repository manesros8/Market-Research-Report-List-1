<p><strong>The Road Bike Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Clincher Bike Tyres</li><li>Tubular Bike Tyres</li><li>Tubeless Bike Tyres</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The road bike tyre market features three main types: clincher, tubular, and tubeless tyres. Clincher tyres are the most common, featuring an inner tube that is held in place by the tyre's bead against the rim. Tubular tyres are stitched around an inner tube and glued onto the rim, preferred by racers for their performance and reduced risk of pinch flats. Tubeless tyres eliminate the inner tube, allowing for lower pressure and improved puncture resistance, gaining popularity among serious cyclists for their versatility.</p></p>
