<p><p>The garbage disposal market primarily features two types: batch feed and continuous feed. Batch feed disposals operate by loading a set amount of waste at once, requiring a lid to be securely closed before activation, making them safer for households with children. Conversely, continuous feed models allow for waste to be added continuously while running, providing convenience during meal preparation. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, balancing safety and efficiency based on kitchen habits and needs.</p></p>
