<p><p>The Electrician Rubber Gloves market is categorized into two primary types: low voltage and high voltage gloves. Low voltage gloves are designed for tasks involving electrical systems under 1,000 volts, providing essential protection for electricians against electric shocks and minor burns. In contrast, high voltage gloves are engineered for use in environments with electrical systems exceeding 1,000 volts, offering superior insulation and protection against severe electric shocks. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety during electrical work.</p></p>
