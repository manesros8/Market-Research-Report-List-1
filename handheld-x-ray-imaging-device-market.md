<p><strong>The Handheld X Ray Imaging Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DR</li><li>CR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld X-ray imaging device market is primarily divided into two types: Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Radiography (CR). DR utilizes digital sensors to capture images directly, providing immediate results and enhanced image quality. In contrast, CR employs a phosphor plate to capture X-ray images, which are later converted into digital format using a reader. Both technologies aim to improve imaging efficiency and portability, catering to various medical applications such as dentistry, orthopedics, and emergency care.</p></p>
