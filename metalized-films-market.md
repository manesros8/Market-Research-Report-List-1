<p><p>Metalized films are thin layers of plastic coated with metal, typically aluminum, to enhance properties like reflectivity, barrier protection, and aesthetics. **Gloss Voided Metalized Films** combine high gloss with a voided layer for opacity and light diffusion. **Reflective Metalized Films** offer high reflectivity for applications like packaging and insulation. **Transparent Metalized Films** retain clarity while adding metallic sheen for decorative or functional uses. **Voided Metalized Films** feature micro-voids for opacity, light diffusion, and improved printability, often used in labels and packaging.</p></p>
