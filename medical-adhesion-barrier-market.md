<p><ul><li>Film Formulation</li><li>Gel Formulation</li><li>Liquid Formulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical adhesion barrier market comprises three primary formulations: film, gel, and liquid. Film formulations are thin, flexible membranes that prevent tissue adhesion following surgery by maintaining a physical barrier. Gel formulations provide a viscous consistency, offering lubrication and protection to surgical sites while adhering to tissues. Liquid formulations are fluid in nature, enabling easy application and coverage of irregular surfaces, effectively reducing post-operative adhesions. Each type serves specific clinical needs, enhancing patient outcomes and promoting faster recovery.</p></p>
