<p><p>The Waste Heat to Power market encompasses various technologies to convert waste heat into electricity. The Steam Rankine Cycle utilizes water as a working fluid, generating power from high-temperature heat sources. Organic Rankine Cycle employs organic fluids with lower boiling points, optimizing energy recovery from lower temperature sources. The Kalina Cycle blends two working fluids, typically water and ammonia, enhancing efficiency by utilizing varying heat sources. Each cycle offers distinct advantages depending on temperature ranges and applications in industrial processes.</p></p>
