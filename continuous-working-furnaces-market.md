<p><p>Continuous working furnaces are essential in industries for efficient heating and processing. Pusher-type continuous furnaces utilize a series of pusher mechanisms to transport materials through the heating zone. Walking-beam continuous furnaces employ a beam system that alternates between supporting and moving workpieces, promoting uniform heating. Roller type heat treatment furnaces utilize rotating rollers to convey products through controlled temperature zones, enhancing treatment efficiency. Other market types may include shuttle, rotary, and tunnel furnaces, serving niche applications and specific heating requirements.</p></p>
