<p><p>A Negative Ion Air Purifier is a device designed to improve indoor air quality by releasing negatively charged ions, which attach to airborne particles like dust, pollen, and smoke, causing them to clump together and fall to the ground. This process helps reduce allergens and pollutants in the air, promoting a healthier living environment. </p><p>The Negative Ion Air Purifier Market is experiencing notable growth, driven by increasing awareness of air quality issues and the health benefits associated with ionization technology.
