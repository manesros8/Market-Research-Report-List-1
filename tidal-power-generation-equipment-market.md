<p><p>Tidal power generation equipment includes several market types based on design and functionality. </p><p>1. **Single-library Unidirectional Station**: This system captures tidal flow in one direction, harnessing energy more efficiently during rising or falling tides.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Single-library Bi-directional Station**: This setup allows energy extraction during both incoming and outgoing tides, optimizing energy capture.</p><p>3. **Double-library Bi-directional Station**: This advanced system maximizes tidal energy by utilizing multiple turbines to generate power from both tidal directions, enhancing overall efficiency and output.</p></p>
