<p><p>The swimming costume dryer market features two primary types: rotation type and drum type. Rotation type dryers use a spinning mechanism to circulate air, effectively drying swimsuits while minimizing fabric damage. In contrast, drum type dryers operate using a rotating drum that gently tumbles the costumes, allowing for even drying and reduced creasing. Both types cater to various consumer needs, focusing on efficiency, fabric care, and convenience to enhance the post-swim drying experience while protecting swimwear.</p></p>
