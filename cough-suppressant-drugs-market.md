<p><p>The cough suppressant drugs market is categorized into two primary types: dry cough and wet cough medications. Dry cough medicines typically contain ingredients that inhibit the cough reflex, providing relief from nonproductive coughs often caused by irritants or allergies. In contrast, wet cough medications aim to alleviate symptoms of productive coughs, which involve mucus production. These products may contain expectorants to help clear phlegm or suppressants to reduce the urge to cough, catering to different patient needs and conditions.</p></p>
