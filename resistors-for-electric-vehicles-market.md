<p><p>In the electric vehicle market, resistors serve various functions. Voltage limiting resistors protect sensitive components by regulating voltage levels, preventing damage from electrical spikes. Other resistors include power resistors, which manage heat and energy distribution, and feedback resistors, which ensure system stability. Together, these resistors play vital roles in the efficient operation and safety of electric vehicles.</p></p>
