<p><ul><li>Single Face Brake</li><li>Power off Brake</li><li>Particle Brake</li><li>Hysteresis Power Brake</li><li>Multiple Disk Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electro-mechanical brakes encompass various types tailored for specific applications. Single face brakes offer compact designs for straightforward stopping power, while power-off brakes engage automatically when power is cut, providing safety. Particle brakes use magnetic particles for frictionless operation, ideal for precise control. Hysteresis power brakes convert electrical energy into mechanical resistance, ensuring smooth deceleration. Multiple disk brakes involve stacking disks to distribute force and enhance performance, making them suitable for high torque applications. Each type serves distinct operational needs across industries.</p></p>
