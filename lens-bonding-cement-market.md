<p><p>The Lens Bonding Cement market is primarily segmented into two types: Epoxy Cement and Acrylate Cement. Epoxy Cement is known for its strong adhesive properties and durability, making it ideal for bonding optical lenses with high clarity and resistance to environmental factors. Both types cater to various optical manufacturing needs, enhancing lens performance and longevity.</p></p>
