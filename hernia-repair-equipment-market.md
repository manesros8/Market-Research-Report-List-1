<p><p>The hernia repair equipment market primarily includes two key types: hernia mesh and mesh fixators. Mesh fixators are devices that secure the mesh in place, ensuring it remains stable over time. Both types play a critical role in enhancing the effectiveness and safety of hernia repair procedures, addressing various patient needs and surgical techniques.</p></p>
