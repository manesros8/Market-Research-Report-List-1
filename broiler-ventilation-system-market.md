<p><p>The broiler ventilation system market includes various types designed to optimize airflow and maintain ideal conditions in poultry houses. **Ridge Ventilation Systems** use roof-mounted vents for natural airflow, ensuring consistent temperature and air quality. **Length Ventilation Systems** rely on sidewall fans and inlets to circulate air along the house length, suitable for moderate climates. **Tunnel Ventilation Systems** employ high-speed fans at one end and inlets at the other, ideal for hot climates. **Others** include hybrid or customized systems tailored to specific needs.</p></p>
