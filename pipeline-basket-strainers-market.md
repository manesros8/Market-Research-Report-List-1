<p><p>Pipeline basket strainers are essential components in fluid systems, designed to filter out debris and contaminants. The market features two main types: simplex basket strainers, which have a single straining element and require manual cleaning, making them suitable for applications with lower flow rates; and duplex basket strainers, featuring two elements allowing for continuous operation as one basket is cleaned while the other remains in service, ideal for high-flow applications. Each type serves distinct industry needs, enhancing efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
